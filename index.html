カタリナ・レム

Phatthadon Kongnaikhao (Stage Name / Alias: Catalina Rem) is a Thai developer, producer, and cosplayer, known as the creator of dreamplay — a media project blending pop culture, humor, and creativity.

Rem is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Applied Statistics and Data Analytics at King Mongkut’s Institute of Technology Ladkrabang (KMITL), Bangkok, Thailand.

After studying in this field, Rem realized that statistical knowledge alone wasn’t enough to solve the real-world problems of interest. This realization sparked a passion for investment strategies and app development, which now serve as tools to build more meaningful and impactful solutions.

Outside of academics and development, Rem also enjoys cosplaying as a creative outlet and a way to connect with pop culture communities.
